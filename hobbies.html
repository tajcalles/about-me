Hobbies

Video games are a passion of mine as well. I usually play big name titles on PC (Overwatch, Starcraft 2, PUBG), but every now and then I play rhythm games like Guitar Hero and DDR. I love being engaged in something that I can control my fate in. Video games allow me to do that, and they provide me with feedback (via the score) to let me know whether I am succeeding or failing.

I find that life sometimes isn’t black and white. There isn’t a concrete, definite score. At times, you need to think and decide for yourself whether or not you’re doing the right things for you (or not). Playing games on my PC allows me to skip the gray area and escape to something I can control, but doesn’t have lifelong-implications.
