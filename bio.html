About Me

I was born in Provo, UT on August 6th, 1991. My family is from Hawai'i, but I grew up in Provo and Sandy here in Utah. I’ve always liked working with computers and building them, but never actually done a ton of work in programming. The last 3 years of my life were spent at Brigham Young University, where I was going to school to be a speech language pathologist. This last year, I’ve wanted to make a change in my life—hence, here I am at Devpoint Labs!In my spare time, I love to play volleyball, video games, and cook. I’ve been around the sport of volleyball for my entire life, and I started playing competitively when I was 15. It’s great exercise to say the least, and it’s been a fun way for me to meet new people and travel to different places I would have never gone without the sport.

Cooking is also a pastime of mine. I’m not a pro chef, but I’ve worked many food jobs in my life--and actually liked them! My favorite job in the foodservice industry was in Alaska. I was a chef on a train called the Wilderness Express. Me and another chef cooked breakfast, lunch, and dinner for tour guests. The work combined with the beautiful scenery of Alaska made for an experience I will never forget.
